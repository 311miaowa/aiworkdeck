# Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

## Why a CLA?
To maintain the ability to offer AI Workdeck under dual licenses (AGPLv3 and Commercial), we may require contributors to sign a Contributor License Agreement (CLA). This ensures that the project maintainers have the necessary legal rights to relicense contributions for commercial customers without tracking down every individual contributor for permission.

## Current Status
*At this initial stage, we are accepting contributions under the standard GitHub flow implied licensing, but we reserve the right to introduce a formal CLA process as the community grows. Large contributions may be subject to ad-hoc review regarding copyright assignment.*
