# Commercial Licensing

## Overview
AI Workdeck Community Edition is open-source under the **AGPLv3**. This allows for great freedom in research, personal use, and open-source collaboration.

However, AGPLv3 requires that if you provide the software as a service (SaaS) over a network, you must release the source code of your modifications to your users.

**We offer a Commercial License that exempts you from the AGPLv3's copyleft requirements.**

## When Do You Need a Commercial License?
You likely need a commercial license if:
- You want to use AI Workdeck Kernel in a **closed-source commercial SaaS** product.
- You are delivering a **proprietary on-premise solution** to customers and cannot share the source code.
- You need **legal assurances** or indemnification not provided by the open-source license.
- You require **dedicated technical support** and SLA.

## FAQ
**Q: Can I use the Community Edition for internal company tools?**
A: Yes, as long as you comply with AGPLv3. Generally, internal use that isn't exposed to external users doesn't trigger distribution clauses, but you should consult your legal counsel.

**Q: If I write a plugin, do I need a commercial license?**
A: If the plugin runs in the same process and relies on the kernel, it may be subject to AGPLv3 viral effects. If you want to keep your plugin proprietary while selling a service based on it, a Commercial License is recommended.
