# Image Prompts for AI Workdeck Website

This document contains "Prompt Master" level prompts to generate high-quality, style-consistent images for the website's highlight cards.

## 1. Hero Section Main Image (Dashboard)
**Theme**: Professional, High-Tech, Integrated, "Command Center".
**Location**: `website/public/images/hero-dashboard.png`
**Concept**: A slightly angled, high-fidelity view of the "AI Workdeck" software interface running on a sleek glass screen. It should look like the "Iron Man" interface of document management.

**Prompt**:
> 3D product render, isometric view, high-tech software interface displayed on a floating glass pane. The interface shows a "Document Intelligence" dashboard: sleek data visualization widgets, a central document editor with AI analysis sidebar (glowing mint green highlights), and file management tree on the left. The color palette is professional white, soft gray, and specific mint green (#3B8262) accents for the "AI" elements. The glass pane has a subtle thickness and premium edge refraction. Soft depth of field blurs the background. Lighting is clean, studio-quality, coming from top-left. Ultra-detailed, 8k resolution, Unreal Engine 5 style. No text, just UI shapes.

## 2. AI Agent Card (不二 Agent)
**Theme**: Efficiency, Intelligence, Precision, Mint Green.
**Location**: `website/public/images/card-ai-agent.png`
**Concept**: A visual metaphor of AI handling complexity—sorting chaos into order with a calm, mint-green glow.

**Prompt**:
> 3D abstract composition, isometric view, high-tech minimalism. A glowing mint green (#3B8262) neural network knot untying itself into straight, organized data streams. The background is a clean, bright off-white laboratory setting with soft, diffuse lighting. Glassmorphism textures, frosted glass elements floating. The "knot" represents complexity, and the "straight lines" represent the streamlined output. Subtle golden ratio composition. Ultra-detailed, 8k resolution, Unreal Engine 5 render style, soft shadows, ambient occlusion. No text.

## 3. Workdeck Card (一站式 Workdeck)
**Theme**: Integration, All-in-One, Blue.
**Location**: `website/public/images/card-workdeck.png`
**Concept**: A futuristic, holographic desktop ("Workdeck") where multiple tools are fused into a single, sleek interface layer.

**Prompt**:
> 3D abstract workspace, isometric view. A translucent, holographic "deck" or slab floating in the center. Resting on it are minimalist 3D icons representing "Voice" (waveform), "Vision" (eye), and "Document" (page), but they are merging into the deck surface seamlessly. Primary color is a professional tech blue (#60A5FA) with accents of white. The surface has a matte finish with glossy edges. Soft, cool lighting coming from the top left. Clean, modern, Apple-style aesthetics. No clutter. 8k resolution, Cinema 4D render.

## 4. Plugin Ecosystem Card (插件生态)
**Theme**: Growth, Modularity, Infinite Possibilities, Orange.
**Location**: `website/public/images/card-plugins.png`
**Concept**: A modular structure growing upwards, like a high-tech city or Lego blocks, symbolizing an ever-expanding ecosystem.

**Prompt**:
> 3D modular structure, isometric view. Building blocks connecting vividly. A central white core block with new blocks attaching to it—one is vibrant orange (#FB923C), others are translucent white and soft gray. It looks like a growing puzzle or a digital crystal forming. Sparks of connection at the joints. The feeling of "Openness" and "Expansion". Background is minimal white studio. High-gloss plastic and matte ceramic textures mixed. Depth of field effect focusing on the new connection point. 8k, Octane render.
